About
Sanskaar singh

Sanskaar Singh is a rapper representing Jamshedpur Hip-Hop best known
for his varied flow and a sharp vocal range. He has been active in
the Jamshedpur's Hip-Hop scene for more than 3 years now.

"The freedom, to be brutally honest and express the scathing truth about life and people, is what drew me to this rap scene. I try to express my genuine thoughts in their pure,unadulterated form so that I can establish a real and honest connection with my listeners. I'm all about going old school in my art while firmly believing that "Parivartan" is the ultimate truth of our being. So, I try to keep up and experiment with my music to make it more interesting and worthwhile."

Demure small-town upbringing and a yearning to forage into different styles is visible in his recent releases. This was noticed by Rukus Avenue Radio, the largest South Asian radio network on which his track with “6USHIDO” was aired worldwide, he has been featured on The Rolling Stone Magazine. As his popularity is gaining momentum, he has also been featured on Livelee.com, and other Hip-Hop and Indie Magazines.

"I strongly believe in reaching for the sky with my roots still firmly attached to the ground. "
